About Browser Privacy Inspector

Browser Privacy Inspector is a free, private tool that runs entirely inside your browser. It reveals every piece of information websites can collect about you — from your GPU model and installed fonts to WebRTC IP leaks and canvas fingerprints — without sending a single byte to a server.

Key Features

  • Browser Fingerprint – Combined SHA-256 hash from canvas, WebGL, and audio APIs.
  • Hardware Info – CPU cores, device memory, GPU vendor & renderer.
  • WebRTC IP Leak – Detects whether your local IP is exposed through WebRTC STUN.
  • Font Fingerprinting – Counts installed fonts via canvas measurement.
  • Network Details – Connection type, downlink speed, and round-trip time.
  • Permissions Status – Camera, microphone, geolocation, and clipboard access.
  • Privacy Score – A comprehensive 0–100 score with recommendations.
  • 100% Client-Side – No server, no analytics, no data ever leaves your device.
