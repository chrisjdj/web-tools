About QR Code Gen

QR Code Gen is a free, privacy-first QR code generator designed for modern web browsers. Unlike traditional generators, all QR code creation happens entirely on your device. We do not use servers to process your data, ensuring complete privacy and security for your URLs, vCards, WiFi passwords, and other sensitive information.

Key Features

  • 100% Client-Side Processing - No tracking, no analytics, no server storage.
  • Extensive Customization - Adjust colors, gradients, dot styles, and ECC levels.
  • High-Quality Exports - Download in PNG, SVG, JPG, WebP, or PDF formats.
  • Multiple Formats - URL, Text, WiFi, vCard, SMS, WhatsApp, and more.
